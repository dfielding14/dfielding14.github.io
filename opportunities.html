I am actively recruiting motivated students and postdocs to join my research group at NYU. There are opportunities at all levels - undergraduate, graduate, and postdoctoral positions.

Graduate Students

Ph.D. Positions

Prospective graduate students interested in theoretical and computational astrophysics are encouraged to apply to the NYU Physics Ph.D. program. I am looking for students interested in:

  • Galaxy formation and evolution
  • Multiphase gas dynamics
  • Turbulence and mixing in astrophysical systems
  • Cosmic ray physics
  • Numerical simulations and high-performance computing

How to Apply: Apply through the NYU Physics graduate admissions portal. Mention my name in your application if you're interested in working with me.

Postdoctoral Researchers

Postdoc Positions

I have openings for postdoctoral researchers to work on projects related to galaxy formation, circumgalactic media, and cosmic ray transport. Positions typically include:

  • Competitive salary and benefits
  • Travel support for conferences
  • Access to high-performance computing resources
  • Opportunities for independent research
  • Mentoring and career development support

To Apply: Send me an email with your CV, research statement, and contact information for three references.

Undergraduate Students

Research Opportunities

Undergraduate students at NYU interested in astrophysics research are welcome to join my group. Projects can range from data analysis to running simulations to developing theoretical models. No prior research experience is required, but strong physics and math backgrounds are helpful.

Opportunities include:

  • Academic year research for credit
  • Summer research positions
  • Senior thesis projects

Getting Started: Email me with your transcript, a brief statement of interest, and any relevant coursework or experience.
